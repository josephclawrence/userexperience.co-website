FAQ

How long will my audit take?

For our most popular 'Quick Wins' audit, you can expect the finished review in 2 - 5 working days. For larger audits we will inform you up front of the delivery date, before you confirm that you would like to proceed.

How does payment work?

We invoice for 50% of the cost up front, and then charge the remaining 50% on completeion of the audit.

What format are the audits in?

You will receive a beautiful PDF document, with all issues and solutions included.

Do you do user testing?

Whilst we do not perform in depth, full scale user testing, we run all of our proposed solutions through a brief series of tests with 'ordinary' folk who are not web designers, or developers. Our audits are primarily expert reviews.

Can we white label / private label your audits/reviews?

You certainly can! If you would like to provide our audits to your clients under your own branding, and sell them on at your own price, just let us know and send through your logo.

What if we have questions after looking through the completed audit?

You are welcome to contact us with any clarifications about anything in your audit - our team will be happy to help.
